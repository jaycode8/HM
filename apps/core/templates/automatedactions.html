AUTOMATED ACTIONS

Irrigation

Irrigation Control

It maintains the ideal pH level in the soil by automatically applying the right type and amount of fertilizer or pH stabilizer

Key Features

  • Turns irrigation systems on/off based on moisture levels
  • Prevents under or over watering
  • Checks water tank levels before activation

How It Works

  1. Soil moisture sensors constantly monitor the ground
  2. When moisture drops below a set threshold, the system;
      • Checks if the water tank has enough water
      • Confirms there is no rain forecast
  3. If the conditions are met, it triggers the irrigation pump to water your crops.
  4. Once the soil is adequately moist again, the system turns off the irrigation.

pH Adjustment

pH Adjustment Control

This system waters your crops automatically based on real-time soil moisture readings

Key Features

  • Monitors soil pH in real time
  • Applies fertilizers or neutralizing agents when pH goes out of optimal range
  • Matches the correct fertilizer based on inventory and crop needs

How It Works

  1. pH measures the acidity or alkalinity of the soil
  2. When pH moves outside the optimal range, the system;
    • Checks the inventory for available pH adjusting substances
    • Determines the right dose based on soil condition and crop type
  3. It then activates a dispenser to apply the correct substance into the soil

Pesticide

Pesticide Control

It protects crops by detecting early signs of pest presence and applying pesticides automatically when needed

Key Features

  • Detects abnormal chemical changes or pest-indicative patterns.
  • Sprays pesticides from inventory when necessary.
  • Follows set safe thresholds to avoid overuse.

How It Works

  1. Special sensors detect abnormal chemical patterns in the soil or leaves that indicate pest activity (or combine with image recognition in advanced setups).
  2. When pests are detected:
    • The system checks available pesticides in the inventory.
    • Selects the correct type and amount based on the pest and crop.
  3. It triggers a sprayer to treat only the affected area.
